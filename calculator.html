Smart Budgeting Tips

Practical advice to stretch your dollar further as a FAU student.

Follow the 50/30/20 Rule

A classic framework for allocating your take-home income:

  • 50% on needs — rent, food, utilities, transport
  • 30% on wants — dining out, entertainment, subscriptions
  • 20% on savings & debt repayment
Cut Textbook Costs

Textbooks are a major hidden expense. Smart strategies:

  • Use FAU's S.E. Wimberly Library for free e-reserves
  • Rent instead of buy — try Chegg or VitalSource
  • Check Facebook Marketplace & FAU Facebook groups
  • Look for older editions (often nearly identical)
Maximize Free Resources

Your tuition covers more than classes:

  • Free gym access at the FAU Recreation Center
  • Campus shuttle reduces parking & gas costs
  • Student discount on Microsoft 365 & Adobe
  • Visit FAU Career Center for free resume & job help
